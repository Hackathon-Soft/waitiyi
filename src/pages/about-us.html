About Us


Welcome to Naseeha Institute

Naseeha Institute is a non-profit and non-political educational institute dedicated to reviving Islamic learning and scholarship among the masses by bringing the study of the Quran and the Prophetic Sunnah to students and working professionals in an institutionalised setting.



Our Vision

Naseeha Institute was set up to bring us back to the Prophet’s life story and to realize our Quranic identity. This identity begins with the Shahada but is an arduous journey of submission to our Creator. Inspired by the Great Prophet Noahs Ark, each talk, workshop or course at Naseeha is part of that larger journey in our life which takes us closer to our true purpose: submission to Allah. The only ones who survived at the time of Prophet Noah were the ones who accompanied him on the boat journey i.e. those in his Suhbat (companionship). Our mission at Naseeha is to bring back that system of companionship created by Allah (SWT) for our tazkiya’a.

Our Mission

Our mission is to produce a generation of independent Islamic scholars and thinkers who are at the same time working professionals contributing to society. Our team is committed to bridging the disconnect between mainstream secular education and specialized Islamic studies curriculum being taught at Madrassas. We expect our graduates to be active as role models in their communities, respected both for their knowledge of religion and for the flexibility, realism, and independence of mind with which they apply it. In this way, they can inspire others to combine the practice of Islam with a positive concern to use their personal and professional skills for the benefit of the whole society.

Our History

The journey of life keeps all of us perplexed as we encounter opportunities to do good & evil in varying circumstances. Allahs love for His creation was manifested by sending Prophets which ended with our Beloved Messenger Muhammad (PBUH). In just 23 years, Prophet Muhammad radically changed everything around him & left the world a better place than when he had come into it. This is the story of Islam. A story that began with our Prophet but continues to date with all of us. Living 1400 years away from his time, we have forgotten the true purpose of our journey in this world.
